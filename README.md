# 📈 Sales Insights Dashboard — Regional Market Performance
**Tool:** Power BI &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** Sales Analytics &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Timeline:** Oct 2025 – Nov 2025

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze regional sales performance, identify top markets, and track revenue trends across multiple years to support data-driven sales strategy decisions.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Sales teams managing multiple regional markets struggle to identify which markets drive the most revenue and which customers/products contribute the most value. This dashboard provides a single view to monitor performance and prioritize focus areas.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Total Revenue:** ₹984.81M across all markets (2017–2020)
- **Total Sales Quantity:** 2M units sold
- **Top Market by Revenue:** Delhi — ₹520M (~53% of total revenue)
- **Top Customer:** Electricalsara Stores — ₹0.65M
- **Revenue Trend:** Peak in early 2018, gradual decline through 2020 — signals need for market re-strategy

## 📌 Features

- 📅 **Year & Month Filter** — drill down by 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020
- 🗺️ **Revenue by Market** — bar chart comparing all regional markets
- 📦 **Sales Qty by Market** — quantity comparison across regions
- 📈 **Revenue by Date** — time-series trend line (Jan 2018 – Jan 2020)
- 🏆 **Top 5 Customers** — ranked by revenue contribution
- 🥇 **Top 5 Products** — ranked by sales performance
